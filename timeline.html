Timeline for Research Progress
DPC-4: Foundation and Initial Work
  • Complete literature review and organize references.
  • Collect and preprocess datasets (e.g., cleaning, labeling).
  • Explore and select models for initial experimentation.

  • Train models and fine-tune hyperparameters.

DPC-6: Deployment and Real-Time Analysis
  • Set up real-time pipelines and APIs for deployment.
  • Deploy the model for real-time sentiment analysis.
  • Monitor performance and optimize for real-time use.
DPC-7: Final Refinement and Report Writing
  • Perform final evaluations and make improvements.
  • Write and submit the final research paper.
  • Prepare presentation for the final defense.
